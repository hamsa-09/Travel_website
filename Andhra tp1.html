About Ooty

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a popular hill station in the southern state of Tamil Nadu, India. It is located in the Nilgiri Hills and is known for its pleasant climate, scenic beauty, tea plantations, and colonial charm. Ooty is a favorite destination for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and honeymooners.
